Who We Are

Redefining Luxury
Landscaping

At Aurum Landscapes, we redefine luxury landscaping, blending artistry with nature to create outdoor spaces of unparalleled beauty. Based in the Cotswolds, we specialise in designing and maintaining exquisite gardens for discerning clients who value exceptional quality.

We draw inspiration from the Cotswolds' rich natural beauty, integrating classical and contemporary design elements to create gardens that exude timeless elegance. Our clients entrust us to enhance their estates with harmonious outdoor environments that are both functional and breathtakingly beautiful.

We take pride in our exclusive, client-focused approach, ensuring each garden we design is as unique as the property it adorns.

Meet Our Founder

James Gold

Founder & Principal
Landscape Designer

James Gold has dedicated over three decades to the art of luxury landscaping. Trained at the Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew, and with a postgraduate qualification from the Landscape Institute, James founded Aurum Landscapes in 1994 with a singular vision: to bring the highest level of artistry and craftsmanship to garden design for England's finest country homes.

His work has been recognised with fourteen industry awards, including the prestigious Society of Garden Designers' Award for Excellence, and has been featured in Country Life, House & Garden, and Gardens Illustrated.

"Every garden we create must earn its place in the landscape — it should feel as though it has always been there, and always will be."

The Process

An Art Form in
Four Movements

From the very first conversation to the final flourish — every stage of our process is executed with precision and care.

How We Work

The Aurum Method

Creating a luxury garden is an art form. Our refined design and build process ensures that every project is executed flawlessly, delivering landscapes that are as functional as they are breathtaking.

01

Consultation
& Concept

Every project begins with a private, unhurried consultation at your estate. We take time to understand your vision, lifestyle, and the architectural and historical character of your property. There is no prescribed agenda — this is a conversation about possibility.

Following the site visit, we prepare a detailed concept document outlining our initial ideas, references, and a proposed scope of works.

02

Design
& Planning

Our award-winning landscape architects craft a detailed design plan, incorporating bespoke planting schemes, structural elements, and luxury features. From formal gardens to contemporary retreats, we tailor every detail to your tastes and the character of your estate.

We present the design through hand-drawn masterplans, 3D visualisations, and material samples — all refined through close collaboration with you until every element is perfect.

03

Construction
& Installation

With meticulous project management, we bring the design to life. Our skilled craftsmen, horticulturists, and stonemasons work with the finest materials — Cotswold limestone, handmade brick, seasoned oak, and wrought iron — to ensure a quality that will endure for generations.

We manage the entire construction process with minimal disruption to your household, maintaining clear communication throughout and adhering to agreed timelines.

04

Finishing Touches
& Maintenance

We refine every detail to perfection in the final stage, ensuring that your new landscape is immaculate and every plant is positioned precisely as designed. We also conduct a comprehensive handover, introducing you to your new garden.

Our team also provides an exclusive, bespoke garden management service, ensuring that your estate remains in pristine condition through every season — year after year.
